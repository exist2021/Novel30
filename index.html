My Story: From 10-Year Dream to 30-Day Reality

I had a story burning inside me. A novel called The Bombay Business Club that I desperately wanted to write. But like many aspiring authors, I looked at the mountain ahead and thought: "This will take at least 10 years."

I had no literary training. I wrote blog posts, sure, but a full novel? That seemed like an insurmountable task requiring skills I simply didn't have.

Then I remembered: AI can help with this.

What happened next surprised even me. In just 30 days, I wrote, published, and distributed my complete novel across multiple platforms. And I'm going to show you exactly how I did it.

Part 1: The Writing Workflow

The Foundation: Claude AI

If you're in the content writing space, you already know Claude is the best AI writing platform available. If you don't know it yet, this is your introduction.

Why Claude?

  • Largest context window of any AI (remembers your entire story)
  • Superior writing quality and tone consistency
  • Excellent at maintaining character continuity
  • Best for creative writing projects

My Daily Writing Process

1 Structure Your Novel

Before writing a single word, I broke down my novel into manageable pieces:

  • 12 chapters total
  • Each chapter divided into 4-5 parts
  • Total: ~60 parts to write

This made the impossible feel achievable. Instead of "write a novel," my task became "write one part today."

2 The Audio Narration Method

Here's the secret that made everything flow naturally:

  1. Record yourself telling the story - I used an audio recorder software (you can use any recorder app, or Claude's built-in audio mode)
  2. Narrate one part at a time - Just talk through what happens in that section, as if you're telling a friend
  3. Get the transcript - Most recording software provides this automatically
  4. Feed it to Claude - Copy-paste the transcript with this prompt:
"This is Chapter [X], Part [X] of my novel. Here's my narration: [paste transcript]. Please write this section in a compelling narrative style."

3 The Refinement Process

The first output from Claude was rarely perfect. It took me 4-5 iterations per part to get:

  • The right tone
  • Character voices correct
  • The pacing just right

Don't be discouraged by this. Refinement is where your novel goes from good to great.

4 Maintaining Continuity

Here's the critical part that most people miss:

After every few chapters:

  1. Upload your completed chapters as files to Claude
  2. Create a character sketch document for all major characters
  3. Fine-tune these sketches with Claude's help
  4. Feed this context to Claude before writing new sections

This ensures Claude never forgets your characters, their motivations, or your story's continuity.

Claude's massive context window means it can keep track of your entire novel. This is its superpower.

Part 3: Creating Supporting Content

Deep Dive Podcast with NotebookLM

I created a Deep Dive overview podcast of my novel using NotebookLM's audio generation feature. This wasn't an audiobook of the entire novel, but rather an engaging discussion-style overview that gives potential readers a taste of the story.

What I did:

  • Fed my novel chapters into NotebookLM
  • Generated an AI-hosted "Deep Dive" conversation about the book
  • Embedded the audio podcast on my website
  • Shared it across social media

Results: Almost 200 listens! People love this format because it's like hearing two people discuss your book in an engaging, conversational way.

Deep Dive podcasts are more engaging than video trailers for books. Audio content allows people to listen while multitasking.

WhatsApp & SMS Campaigns with Apple Shortcuts

This is where automation became a game-changer. I configured Apple Shortcuts to automate bulk messaging:

My Apple Shortcuts Setup:

  1. Prepare Excel sheet with mobile numbers and names
  2. Create Shortcut to read Excel data
  3. Auto-send personalized messages via WhatsApp
  4. Auto-send SMS for non-WhatsApp contacts

How it works: The shortcut reads each row from your Excel file, personalizes the message with the contact's name, and automatically sends it via WhatsApp or SMS.

Benefits:

  • Completely free (no bulk SMS costs)
  • Personal touch with name customization
  • Works directly from iPhone
  • Can process hundreds of contacts automatically
Apple Shortcuts for bulk messaging is a hidden gem! While others pay for SMS services, you can reach your entire contact list for free using your existing WhatsApp and SMS.

Common Mistakes I Made (So You Don't Have To)

  1. Not creating character sketches early enough - Start this from Chapter 2 or 3
  2. Writing one chapter in ChatGPT - Stick to one AI platform for consistency
  3. Not testing PDF formats with beta readers sooner - Test early and often
  4. Underestimating the time for platform approvals - Start the Google Play and Apple Books process early
  5. Not setting up email automation earlier - Start building your email list from day one

  3. Start with one part - Narrate it today
  4. Feed it to Claude - Get your first draft
  5. Refine and repeat - 4-5 iterations per part
  6. Set up your marketing - Email lists and automation from day one
